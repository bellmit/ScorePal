About
Umpyr is a website created by Darker Waters LTD (UK). It is intended as a place to come to enter and record your scores as you play the sport of your choice.

There is (will be) a companion app on Android and hopefully other platforms in the future. It is envisioned that you record the score of your game as you play it to help you track and keep accurate scoring statistics as you play. Then as you progress, you can see that change in your game in this site.

As the site grows it may well include groups which you can join, locations to play, more sports, different ways to sign-in, and more as required.
